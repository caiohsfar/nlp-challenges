A U.S. Forces F-15 fighter taking off from Kadena Air Base, Okinawa on a routine training mission crashed about 800 meters from the runway.
The crash site is within the air base's facilities.
The pilot safely ejected without injuries.
The aircraft belonged to the 44th Fighter Squadron of the 18th Wing.
The Defense Facilities Administration Agency (DFAA) asked the command of the U.S. Forces in Japan (USFJ) to take thorough measures for safe management of the U.S. military bases on Okinawa and to conduct a thorough investigation into the cause of the crash.
Investigation details will be made public.
