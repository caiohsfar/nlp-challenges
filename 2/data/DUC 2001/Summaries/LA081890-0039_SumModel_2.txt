Sunday's America's Finest City HomeFed Half Marathon is scheduled to begin at 7am at the Cabrillo Monument atop the Point Loma Peninsula.
It will wind to a finish line in Balboa Park.
The course is considered to be challenging and difficult.
Most elite runners who train in San Diego stay away from the race.
Some competitors have run in the Trib 10K in May, the Coronado Half Marathon in July, the Goodwill Games 10K last month in Seattle, Grandma's Marathon, the New York City Marathon, the Twin Cities Marathon, the San Francisco Marathon, the Nebraska Half Marathon, and marathons in Houston and Boston.
