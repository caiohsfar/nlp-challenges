Liz Taylor has been plagued by health woes since 1945 when she fell from a horse during the filming of "National Velvet".
She has endured about 20 major operations on her back, appendix, eyes and teeth.
Respiratory ailments have been a recurring problem.
She has survived seven rocky marriages to six men, and addictions to alcohol, painkillers and food.
In 1983 and again in 1988 she underwent addiction treatment at the Betty Ford Clinic Her many setbacks have added to her stardom, and she has never attracted more attention than she has been getting during her current hospitalization.
