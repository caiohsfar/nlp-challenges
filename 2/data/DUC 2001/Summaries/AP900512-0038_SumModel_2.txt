Doctors treating Elizabeth Taylor said on Friday that her steady recovery from viral pneumonia had been interrupted by bacterial pneumonia and a yeast infection.
The actress was admitted to Daniel Freeman Marina Hospital on April 9th, suffering from a severe sinus infection.
She was transferred to St. John's Hospital and Health Center when her condition deteriorated.
Doctors later revealed that she was near death on April 22nd.
The secondary bacterial pneumonia often follows viral pneumonia.
Her condition is now improving bur she is expected to remain in the hospital for some time.
