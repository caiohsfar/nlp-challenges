Foreigners continued their domination of the New York City Marathon.
Sunday's race was plagued by heat and humidity, with the temperature reaching 72 and humidity peaking at 66%.
The hottest year in this race's history was 1984 when the temperature reached 79.
Douglas Wakiihuri, a Kenyan who trains in Japan, won overall in 2:12:39.
Poland's Wanda Panfil, who lives in Mexico, was the women's winner in 2:30:45.
The last American men's winner was Alberto Salazar in 1982.
The last American women's champion was Miki Gorman in 1977.
Wakiihuri also won the 1989 London Marathon and the 1990 Commonwealth Games marathon.
