Elizabeth Taylor has recovered from a life-threatening case of pneumonia.
A lung specialist treating her said her life was in danger over the weekend, and that she is over the worst but she is not yet "out of the woods".
There have been consistent rumors about AIDS, but her doctors said that there is nothing to suggest AIDS or cancer.
The actress had earlier denied that she had AIDS.
Marie Burton-Carson, Liza Todd-Tivey, and Christopher and Michael Wilding, her four children from three marriages, have been with her.
