On Sunday Ernesto Beatriz Martinez of Mexico City won the San Diego International Marathon in 2:16:12.
Marie Rollins won the women's race in 2:39:05.
They received $5,000 each.
Second place took $2,500 and third $1,500.
The starting line was on East Mission Bay Drive.
The city doesn't allow vehicles on the course from miles 18-22.
There were 3,056 entries for the full marathon and 2,958 for the half.
Jeffrey Holyfield won the men's half in 1:07:44, Katie Webb the women's half in 1:21:46, Bill Fricke the wheelchair marathon in 1:55:30, and 14-year old Eric Neitzel the wheelchair half in 1:13:14.
