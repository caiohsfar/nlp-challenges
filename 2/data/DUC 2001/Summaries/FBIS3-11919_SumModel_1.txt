There has been widespread international reaction to the assassination of Luis Donaldo Colosio Murrieta, presidential candidate for the Mexican Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI) on March 233 in Tijuana.
The Colombian Government and several presidential candidates expressed their regrets and condemned such violence.
A spokesman for the Cuban Foreign ministry extended the sympathy of the Cuban people to the Mexican Government and people.
In El Salvador leaders of the ruling Arena Party and of the opposition coalition expressed condolences and condemned the cowardly act.
Venezuela's Foreign Minister deplored the assassination and expressed his sadness over the incident.
