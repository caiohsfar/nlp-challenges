In Memphis and other areas up and down the Mississippi Valley people are concerned about the prediction of an earthquake on 3 December along the New Madrid fault.
In some areas, thousands of people are crowding into earthquake survival classes while entrepreneurs are hawking quake insurance, and gas-line safety gadgets.
In Arkansas and Missouri, some schools and business have announced plans to close in early December.
Everybody talks about the San Andreas Fault in California, but the Midwest actually has had three of the most powerful earthquakes on this continent.
In actuality, scientists say that there is no way to predict when an earthquake will occur.
