Clarence Thomas' confirmation hearings for Supreme Court Justice are scheduled to begin in August 1991.
Democrats promise to determine Thomas' views on a number of current important issues, abortion, women's rights, etc.
Civil rights groups intend to oppose him, believing that he is insensitive to the concerns of minorities and the elderly.
He is a firm believer in a bootstrap philosophy.
Clarence Thomas was born on 23 June 1948.
Highlights of his career are: a. B.A., Holy Cross, 1971 b. J.D., Yale, 1974 c. Assistant Attorney General, Missouri, 1974-77 d. Chairman, Equal Employment Opportunity Commission, 1982-1990 e. U.S. Circuit Court of appeals, 1990.
